How Much does it cost to set up a store?

You get access to unlimited inventory, custom shipping, and secure credit card payments. We build you a custom ecommerce website ready to go for a set up fee of $599 and $99/month


What products will my store sell?

Your branded retail store will contain a wide variety of hair products. You will offer some the the finest human hair on the market including Brazilian, malaysian, Indian, Euro hair. You will also have lace wigs, closures, kinky hair and many other products to expand the variety of your store as time goes on and they are launched.The more product you have to offer the more opportunity for sales.


Can I use my own domain name?

Yes. We set up your store with your own web address to further brand your store. We recommend you make your name something easy to remember and tied to your own business.


How long does it take to set up my store?

We have a formalized process to set up your store. Typically to set up your store takes approximately 1 week to get everything set up and ready to take orders.


Can I use my own logo with my store?

Yes. We encourage you to use your own logo in your Branded store. However, a logo is not required to start selling.


Who ships out the products to my customers?

We handle all shipping of orders and put your store name on each packing slip so your customers know it comes directly from you.


How much can I make with my own store?

We have set up our program to enable you to make Thousands of dollars per month. You can make between 20% - 40% of everything sold in our store as a branded retailer.

How do I get paid?

We will deposit your earning directly into your bank account or onto debit card. Payments are made once you reach over $100. Deposits are scheduled for every 2 weeks.

How do you help me get customers for my store?

As a etailer we view you as a team member and our goal is to help you sell as much as possible. We provide your store with an initial SEO (search engine optimization) campaign to get you up and going. We also offer enhanced marketing packages for store owners who want to marketing more heavily and improve their sales immediately. We can help with social media programs and printed marketing materials.


How quickly will products be shipped to my customers?

Some products will be offered for overnight shipping with standard shipping times for other products at 5-7 business days.


How can I personalize my ecommerce site?

We provide you several personalization points to establish your site as your very own and start pushing your brand. You will be able to customize the name, add a logo, add some custom text, and more!


Can I integrate my own blog into my store site?

We recommend you set up a blog to continuously add content about beauty and hair to show your expertise. We then provide a link on your store directly to your blog for customers that want more information about you. Integrating your existing Blog is simple and easy as well.


How often will new products be introduced?

We will be introducing new products on a consistent basis and letting your existing customer base know to continue to drive sales.
